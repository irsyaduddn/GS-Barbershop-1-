2. Login Module
-Enter your email and password according to your role (Customer, Barber, Admin).
-Click "Log In" to access the system.
-You will be redirected to your respective dashboard.

3. Account Registration Module (Customer Only)
(For new users)

-Click "Sign Up Now" on the login page.
-Fill in Full Name, Email, Password, Confirm Password, Phone Number, and Age.
-Click "Register" to create an account.

4. Manage Services Module (Admin)
-Admin can add new grooming services with name, price, and duration.
-Admin can edit or delete existing services.
-Admin can also manage add-on services and link them to main services.

5. Appointment Booking & Management Module (Customer & Barber)
Customer:
-Select desired service, barber, and available time slot.
-Choose add-on services and confirm appointment.
-View upcoming appointments and cancel if needed.

Barber:
-View list of assigned appointments.
-Update appointment status (e.g., Completed).
-View daily schedule and update availability.

6. Barber Availability Management Module (Barber)
-Barbers can block off unavailable dates using the "Take Day Off" feature.
-Manage and delete upcoming available time slots.
-View status of each time slot (Booked or Available).

7. Payment Module
Customer:
-After selecting service, proceed to checkout and confirm details.
-Redirected to Stripe Checkout for secure payment.
-Receive confirmation email after successful payment.

Admin:
-Track all payment records including status (Paid, Refunded).
-Monitor transactions tied to each appointment.

8. Report Generation Module (Admin)
-Admin can generate reports for a selected date range.
-Reports include total appointments, completed/cancelled status, and revenue.
-Use “Export to PDF” to download the report for record keeping or analysis.
